Discovery using the Generic Client

The discovery of other UA products on any given computer will depend upon the availability of a Discovery Server.

This package provides a Local Discovery Server.

Clicking the "Discover Servers" option from the "Discovery" menu will open this simple dialog:

After querying the host specified in the "Host Name" field you will see a list of UA Servers (this client filters to retrieve servers only) products of which you can highlight and select.
